About Apples

Apples are crisp, sweet-tart fruits from the Malus domestica tree, enjoyed fresh or in a wide variety of dishes. Rich in fiber, vitamin C, and antioxidants, they support heart health and digestion. With hundreds of varieties - from tart Granny Smith to sweet Honeycrisp - apples offer diverse flavors and textures year-round.

About Television

A television is an electronic device that receives audio-visual signals to display moving images and sound. Used for entertainment, news, education, and streaming, modern TVs feature high-resolution screens (HD, 4K, 8K), smart apps, and various display technologies like LED, OLED, and QLED.
